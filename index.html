About Me

Hi, I’m Dillu, a passionate photographer and web developer based in Hyderabad. I specialize in candid event photography, family portraits, and studio shoots that feel natural and personal.

With a background in speech therapy and photography, I focus on capturing genuine emotions and unplanned moments that tell a story.

When I’m not behind the camera, I’m designing websites and optimizing online presence for small businesses and clinics.
